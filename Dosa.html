Dosa

Dosa is a popular South Indian dish known for its thin, crispy texture and savory taste. It is a type of fermented crepe made from a batter of rice and urad dal (black gram lentils). Dosas are typically served hot and are often enjoyed with a variety of accompaniments such as chutneys, sambar, and spicy potato filling.

Ingredients

2 cups rice (preferably parboiled rice or idli rice)
1/2 cup urad dal (split black gram lentils)
1/4 teaspoon fenugreek seeds (optional, for better fermentation).
